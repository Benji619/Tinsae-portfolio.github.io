About me

Hi there! My name is Tinsae Abiyo, and I'm a dedicated advocate for youth and families based in Shoreline, WA. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Sociology from Central Washington University, where I developed a deep understanding of social dynamics and community engagement. Currently, I serve as a Youth and Family Advocate at Vision House, where I passionately support and empower individuals and families facing challenges. It's immensely fulfilling to be able to make a positive impact on the lives of others, especially in the realm of education and social support. Beyond my professional endeavors, I find joy and fulfillment in various hobbies. As a gospel singer, I express myself through music and find solace in uplifting melodies. Additionally, I enjoy sharing my thoughts and experiences through blog writing, allowing me to connect with others and inspire meaningful conversations. In all aspects of my life, whether it's through my work or my hobbies, I am driven by a genuine passion for helping others and making a difference in the world around me.
